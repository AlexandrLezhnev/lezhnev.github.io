Adobe, is an American software company. The headquarters is located in San Jose (California). Founded in February 1982 by John Warnock and Charles Geschke in Mountain View, California. They founded Adobe after they left Xerox PARC in order to continue developing PostScript and extract commercial profit from it. The name comes from Adobe Creek, a stream in California lying between the settlements of Palo Alto and Mountain View. Kevin Lynch it is Technology director joined the company in 2005 and participated in the development of cloud products and promoted the idea of multi-screen services. On March 22, 2013, Kevin moved to Apple to develop cloud products on a subscription-based distribution model. In June 2014, the company introduced the Ink pen and the Slide line.
